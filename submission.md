# Submission

## Guidelines for Authors

- Submissions should align with TALE's focus on technology-enhanced assessment and learning environments.
- We welcome empirical studies, conceptual analyses, and systematic or critical reviews.
- Manuscripts should include a clear abstract, keywords, method or analytical framing, findings or argument, and implications.
- Authors are encouraged to report ethical approval and data governance practices where relevant.
- Please ensure all references and citations are complete and consistent before submission.
